---

When asked to zoom out on a codebase, module, or problem, provide a high-level overview.

## What to cover

- **What is this thing?** One-sentence purpose.
- **Where does it fit?** How it relates to other parts of the system.
- **What are its boundaries?** What it does and doesn't handle.
- **Key files or entry points** — where to start reading.
- **Key abstractions** — the ideas that hold it together.
- **Surprising complexity** — anything that looks harder than it should be.

## Purpose

The goal is to build a mental model quickly. Don't dive into details. Give the reader enough context to navigate the code themselves.
